# Privacy Policy for NEF

**Last Updated:** 11 March 2026

This privacy policy explains what data NotEnoughFakepixel collects, why it is collected, and how it is used. We believe in keeping our open-source community informed. By using this mod, you consent to the data collection practices described below. 

### 1. What Data We Collect
When you play Minecraft with NotEnoughFakepixel installed, the mod automatically sends a small analytics payload. This data includes:
* **Your Minecraft Username:** Used purely to count unique players and prevent duplicate analytics data.
* **The Server IP You Are Connected To:** We collect the IP of the Fakepixel you are playing on (not your personal/home IP address) to verify that the mod is being used on our intended server and avoid usage out of it.
* **The NEF version you are using:** NotEnoughFakepixel version used at the moment. We use this specifically to check version usage and updates.

### 2. Why We Collect This Data
We use this data strictly for basic analytics and development purposes. It helps us understand:
* How many active, unique players are using the mod.
* Whether players are actually using the mod on our designated server.
* Which versions of our mod are currently in use, so we know when to stop supporting older versions.
* The safest hours to deploy an update whether in the mod or in Skyblock.

### 3. Data Storage and Protection
The data is sent directly to our private server hosted at [Render](https://render.com/). 
* We do not track your personal home IP address.
* We do not store any data, and process it instantly only for graphic purposes.

### 4. Third-Party Sharing
We **do not** sell, trade, or share your data with any third parties. It is used exclusively by the developer(s) of NEF.

### 5. Contact
If you have any questions or concerns about this data collection, please open an issue on this GitHub repository or contact me at @im2pb.
